hello! i'm currently on a gap year between undergrad (MIT '24 physics) and grad school (princeton astrophysics). i'm currently researching at the [max planck institute for gravitational physics (albert einstein institute)](https://www.aei.mpg.de/person/137604/25873) on a [fulbright](https://us.fulbrightonline.org/countries/europe-and-eurasia/germany) fellowship, studying dark siren methods for measuring the hubble constant with gravitational wave sources. broadly, my research interests include cosmology, gravity, and astrophysical transients. i'm a [2023 astronaut scholar](https://astronautscholarship.org/scholars.html) and a 2023 [ligo surf](https://labcit.ligo.caltech.edu/LIGO_web/students/SURF/). 

in high school, i did the [astrophysics olympiad](https://usaaao.org/about/history/) and science olympiad. something i'm still heavily involved in is writing astronomy tests and pedagogical resources for [science olympiad](/scioly/). outside of school and research, i think about life, [ramble about it sometimes](/blog/), enjoy playing video games (mostly indie games and VALORANT), and [watch a lot of anime](https://anilist.co/user/stellarevolution/animelist).
