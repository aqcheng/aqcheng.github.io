hello! i'm a rising junior at MIT studying physics, math, and astronomy. something you should know about me is that i like (love) astrophysics! i'm broadly interested in extragalactic astrophysics and cosmology, and i currently do research on binary black hole population inference with gravitational wave data. i'm a [2023 astronaut scholar](https://astronautscholarship.org/scholars.html) and a 2023 [ligo surf](https://labcit.ligo.caltech.edu/LIGO_web/students/SURF/).

in high school, i did the [astrophysics olympiad](https://usaaao.org/about/history/) and science olympiad. something i'm still heavily involved in is writing astronomy tests and pedagogical resources for [science olympiad](/scioly/). if you're a high school student, feel free to reach out! if not, i love meeting new people anyway, so feel free to dm me on [twitter](https://twitter.com/aqc___). 

outside of school and research, i think about life, [ramble about it sometimes](/blog/), enjoy playing video games (mostly indie games and VALORANT), and [watch a lot of anime](https://anilist.co/user/stellarevolution/animelist).
