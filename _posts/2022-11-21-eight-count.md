one. <br>
above me, the blank canvas is a distant heaven; <br>
i count its ripples like stars <br> <br>

two. <br>
around me, the sky-blue walls are a ladder; <br>
i climb up with sticky hands <br> <br>

three. <br>
behind me, a cold breeze; the window is open <br>
but i am not a dove <br> <br>

four. <br>
in the corner, a mirror shows my worth. lace, gift-wrapped <br>
soft clay ham and a hole <br> <br>

five. <br>
by my side, some body familiar <br> <br>

six. <br>
the yellow ceiling-light glares down like a dentist <br> <br>

seven. <br>
on the wall, the centipede clock crawls, <br> <br>

a faithless metronome
