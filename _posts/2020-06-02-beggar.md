The cars come to a stop <br>
Gently, or with a screech. <br>
I see a palette. <br>

<span style="color:Red">Red.</span> <br>

Could you spare me a second, <br>
And some change? <br>
Where are you going, <br>
And where will you go? <br>

Some of them are kind. <br>
They roll down the window, <br>
and I can add another book to the shelf. <br>
All things come and go. <br>

<span style="color:Green">Green.</span> <br>

The cars start again <br>
In silence, or with a rumble. <br>
I wave; there’ll be more. <br>

But if I had known that would be the last time I would see you, <br>
I would have said goodbye.
