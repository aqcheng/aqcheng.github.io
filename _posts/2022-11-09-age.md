i was wandering down the stairs <br>
going somewhere (anywhere) <br>
then, i don’t know when, <br>
i started running and now <br>
with every step that i debase myself <br>
my chalk-white bones <br>
crumble and the perfume-foul flesh <br>
that kisses them melts away <br>
a little bit like candle wax. <br>
hey you, i’m in a hurry, don’t <br>
get in my way—soon, my <br>
curtain skin will rise <br>
to burn out spectacularly— <br>
and when i finally sink into the <br>
quiet Earth, my skeleton <br>
a beautifully broken doll, <br>
my jaw will distend sideways <br>
in the warm hearth of my <br>
grave, in mock surprise. <br>
